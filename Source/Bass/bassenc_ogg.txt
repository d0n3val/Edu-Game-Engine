What's the point?
=================
BASSenc_OGG is an extension to the BASSenc add-on that allows BASS channels
to be Ogg Vorbis encoded, with support for OGGENC options.


Requirements
============
BASS 2.4 and BASSenc 2.4.12 or above are required.


Using BASSenc_OGG
=================
The usage information in the BASS.TXT file (from the BASS package) is also
applicable to BASSenc_OGG and other add-ons.
